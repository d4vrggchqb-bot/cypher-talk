FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How do I reset my password?

+
Click "Forgot Password?" on the login page. You'll need to provide either your username or email address. A password reset link will be sent to your registered email address with the @matrix.com domain.

Note: If you used a username without an email, your default email is username@matrix.com

Can I sign in anonymously?

+
Yes! Click the "Sign in Anonymously" button to generate a temporary guest account. Anonymous accounts:
  • Are automatically generated
  • Have limited features
  • Expire after 24 hours of inactivity
  • Cannot be recovered if lost
For permanent access, create a regular account.

Why is my username not available?

+
Usernames must be unique across the system. If a username is taken, try:
  • Adding numbers (e.g., neo123)
  • Using underscores (e.g., the_one)
  • Combining words (e.g., matrixRider)
  • Making it longer (minimum 3 characters)
Common names like "neo", "trinity", and "morpheus" are usually taken.

What are the password requirements?

+
For optimal security:
  • Minimum 8 characters (recommended 12+)
  • At least one uppercase letter
  • At least one number
  • At least one special character
  • Not based on personal information
The system shows real-time strength indicators. Strong passwords are recommended.

Is my data secure?

+
YES. We implement military-grade encryption:
  • End-to-end encryption for all messages
  • Zero-knowledge authentication
  • No third-party data sharing
  • Automatic session expiration
  • No logs kept of your activities
Remember: "What happens here, stays here..."
